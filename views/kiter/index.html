About us

We are a surf school with almost fifteen years of experience, located in Adícora, Falcón. We are defined by good vibes, closeness and professionalism. The heat of the peninsula will hug you, and we will baptize you through the waves. We teach kitesurf, windsurf and foil, and also we are a kite beach club.

EXPLORE ADICORA

Located in the state of Falcón, Venezuela, Adicora is a windsurfing paradise. It is characterized by its endless and reddish sunsets, friendly people with good vibes who are willing to help you and teach you the best sport in the world, kitesurfing.
